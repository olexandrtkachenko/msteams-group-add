# Privacy Policy for MS Teams Group Add Extension

**Last Updated:** November 25, 2024

## Overview
MS Teams Group Add ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles user data.

## Data Collection
**The Extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Specifically:
- ✅ No user data is collected
- ✅ No browsing history is tracked
- ✅ No personal information is stored
- ✅ No data is sent to external servers
- ✅ No analytics or tracking tools are used
- ✅ No cookies are created

## Data Processing
The Extension processes data **locally in your browser only**:

### What the Extension Does:
1. **Reads** the email addresses and names you paste
2. **Processes** this data to add members to MS Teams
3. **Displays** progress and statistics
4. **Deletes** all data when you close the popup

### What the Extension Does NOT Do:
- Does NOT save your data anywhere
- Does NOT send data to any server
- Does NOT share data with third parties
- Does NOT track your usage
- Does NOT store any information

## Permissions Explanation

The Extension requires the following permissions:

### `activeTab`
- **Purpose:** Access the currently active MS Teams tab
- **Usage:** Required to interact with the MS Teams page
- **Data Access:** Only the active tab, only when you use the Extension

### `scripting`
- **Purpose:** Inject scripts to automate member addition
- **Usage:** Required to fill forms and click buttons on MS Teams
- **Data Access:** Only MS Teams pages, only during active use

### `host_permissions` (teams.microsoft.com)
- **Purpose:** Work specifically with MS Teams web version
- **Usage:** Required to interact with MS Teams interface
- **Data Access:** Only MS Teams domain, no other websites

## Data Security
Since the Extension does NOT collect or store any data, there are no data security concerns. All processing happens:
- Locally in your browser
- In real-time
- With no storage
- With no transmission

## Third-Party Services
The Extension does NOT use any third-party services:
- No analytics (Google Analytics, etc.)
- No crash reporting
- No advertising
- No external APIs
- No cloud storage

## Children's Privacy
The Extension does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.
